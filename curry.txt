Chop 1 onion and 1 head of garlic fine
Heat a 1/4 cup oil in large dutch oven pan
Add onion and garlic - lower heat cook apx 5min
Add 6 tablespoons Madras (yelwow) curry powder
Add 1 tablespoon regular cumin
Add 1 tsp Dave's if you can get it - Garmasala
Fry for about 2 - 3 min low heat - stir const
Add meat of choice or veggies (meat or veg in 1" cube best)
Fry 5 min stir const
Add boiling water or chiken stock
Lower heat - simmer - 15min veg, 30-40chick, 60-90 beef lamb goat
